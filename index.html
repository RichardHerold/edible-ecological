The Story

Edible Ecologicals transforms the complex narrative of climate change into a visceral, sensory experience through the medium of ice cream. This artistic exploration captures the delicate balance of New England's forest ecosystems, translating environmental data and ecological shifts into flavors that tell the story of our changing landscape.


As temperatures rise and precipitation patterns shift, the iconic sugar maples and paper birches of New England face an uncertain future. This project invites participants to literally taste these changes, creating an intimate connection between our palates and the planet's transformation.


By partnering with local syrup producers and artisanal ice cream makers, we bridge the gap between traditional forest knowledge and contemporary climate science, offering a unique perspective on environmental change that engages all the senses.

The Flavors

Maple Migration

A tribute to the sugar maple's northward journey, this flavor captures the bittersweet reality of shifting forest boundaries. Rich maple syrup from traditional New England producers is balanced with subtle notes that evoke the stress and resilience of these iconic trees.

Tasting notes: Deep caramel sweetness with hints of wood smoke and a touch of salt, representing the trees' struggle and adaptation.

Birch Resilience

Celebrating the adaptable paper birch, this unique flavor showcases the distinctive taste of birch syrup—earthy, complex, and unlike anything you've experienced. It tells the story of a species finding new ecological niches as the climate shifts.

Tasting notes: Molasses-like depth with savory undertones, balanced by cream and subtle berry notes, reflecting the birch's pioneering spirit.

The Process

Research & Foraging

We began by studying climate data and forest health reports, working with ecologists to understand the specific impacts on New England's tree species. This research informed our flavor profiles and narrative.

Syrup Sourcing

We partnered with traditional syrup producers who have witnessed firsthand the changes in sap flow patterns and season timing, incorporating their generational knowledge into our project.

Flavor Development

Working with New City Microcreamery's expert artisans, we translated our research into flavor profiles that balance scientific accuracy with sensory delight, creating ice creams that are both educational and delicious.

Artistic Expression

Each batch is crafted with intention, turning data points into taste experiences. The presentation and serving ritual become part of the artwork, inviting contemplation and conversation.
